**Tiles (Student Homepage)**

Q: Where can I search for classes?
A: My Academics tile > Search tab.

Q: Where can I view my degree audit?
A: My Academics tile > My Academics tab > Academic Requirements. Also accessible from the Registration tile.

Q: Where can I make a fee payment?
A: My Finance tile > Make a Payment. Also in Student Center > Finances > Make a Payment.

Q: Where can I see my account balance?
A: My Finance tile > Account Inquiry, or Student Center > Finances > Account Inquiry.

Q: Where can I update my emergency contacts?
A: Demographic Details tile > Personal Information tab > Emergency Contacts.

Q: Where can I download my fee receipt?
A: Student Center > Finances > Fees Receipt.

Q: Where can I check my exam schedule?
A: Student Center > Academics dropdown > Exam Schedule.

Q: Where can I view my grades?
A: Student Center > Academics dropdown > Grades.

**Navigator Menu**

Q: Where can I view my enrollment appointment date?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Enrollment > Enrollment Dates.

Q: Where can I apply for graduation?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Degree Progress / Graduation > Apply for Graduation.

Q: Where can I run a What-If report?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Degree Progress / Graduation > View What-if Report. Also under My Academics tile > My Academics tab.

Q: Where can I set up direct deposit?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Campus Finances > Enroll in Direct Deposit.

Q: Where can I track my Minor program progress?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Admissions > Minor Program Progress Details. Also under My Academics tile.

Q: Where can I submit an MoU proposal?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > MoU Proposal.

Q: Where can I view my weekly class schedule?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Enrollment > My Weekly Schedule.

Q: Where can I accept my offer of admission?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Student Admission > Accept Admission.

Q: Where can I view my No Dues clearance status?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Records and Enrollment > No Dues.

Q: Where can I find pending tasks assigned to me by the university?
A: NavBar > Navigator > Self Service > Campus Personal Information > To Do List. Also visible in the Student Center.

---

**System Features**

Q: Where can I sign out of the portal?
A: Via the Actions Menu (three-dot icon) in the top navigation bar > Sign Out.

Q: Where can I bookmark a page for quick access later?
A: Use the Actions Menu > Add to Favorites, then access it later via NavBar > My Favorites.

Q: Where can I change my password?
A: NavBar > Navigator root > Change My Password.
